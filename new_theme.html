Join us for the exciting El Día 2024 World (Global) Café!

We're thrilled to invite you to participate in the World (Global) Café session during El Día del Agua y la Atmósfera on March 19, 2024, from 8 am to 6 pm at the University of Arizona’s ENR2 Building.

In the afternoon, we'll gather for a dynamic World Café session where students and attendees will come together to discuss, envision, connect, and innovate the future of Hydrology and Atmospheric Sciences. We'll kickstart this workshop session by framing questions that encourage active participation and diverse perspectives. Check flyer for more information

Your input will be invaluable as we delve into the most pressing challenges and opportunities facing our field. To ensure we capture your insights effectively, we've prepared a Google Form where you can confidentially share your thoughts. Please take a moment to complete the form before the workshop. You can access the form here: Café Form.

Here are the questions we'll explore:

  1. What do you think the major emphasis of a re-imagined HAS undergraduate degree should be?
  2. If you were tasked with defining the core curriculum for all MS programs in hydrogeology, what are the most important things to include? What should be left out that might be surprising? Do you have recruiting ideas for a redefined MS in hydrogeology?
  3. How can we encourage more students to consider HAS as an undergraduate major?
  4. We are hoping to raise funds to help to support a highly competitive professor of practice position to run the one year MS in hydrogeology. Can you propose any possible sources of financial support that we might not have considered?

Your contributions will shape our discussions and pave the way for innovative solutions. We look forward to your participation in shaping the future of Hydrology and Atmospheric Sciences at El Día!

For more information about the event and to secure your spot, visit our website. Don't forget to register to enjoy a complimentary lunch.

Thank you for your engagement, and we can't wait to see you at El Día!
